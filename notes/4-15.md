An additive subgroup of $\mathbb{R}$ is a set $G \subseteq \mathbb{R}$ with the following properties:

1. $0 \in G$
2. $x, y \in G \Rightarrow x + y \in G$
3. $x \in G \Rightarrow -x \in G$
